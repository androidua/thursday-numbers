📊 How Each Strategy Works

Hot Numbers: Picks from the 10 most-drawn main balls historically (9, 7, 17, 11, 19…). Based on the idea that some numbers appear more often.

Cold Numbers: Picks from the 10 least-drawn balls (31, 13, 26, 29…). Based on the idea that they're "overdue" — though randomness means this isn't actually true.

Mix Strategy: Combines 5 hot + 5 cold, giving a balanced statistical spread.

True Random: Every number has equal probability — exactly how the actual draw works. Statistically the same as any other strategy.

⚠️ Important: Powerball is a game of pure chance. Each draw is independent and random. Past results have zero statistical influence on future draws. This tool is for entertainment only.
